Theory test

If you have had a number of lessons you may be starting to feel more comfortable behind the wheel, you might even feel that you are ready to take your practical test.

There is just one little hurdle to overcome first; a theory test. Don’t worry it isn't too difficult, just as long as you prepare properly. I will help you build your knowledge through our driving lessons and I also have a few tips and links to help you prepare in your own time.

The theory test is made up of two parts; multiple-choice and hazard perception. You will need to pass both parts to pass the theory test.

Once you have achieved this we can then agree a suitable time for you to request a practical driving test.
